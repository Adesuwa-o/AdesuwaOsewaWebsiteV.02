About

I'm Adesuwa

Problem Solver. Radio Presenter. DJ.

I’m a Problem Solver for creative management consultancy B+A, consulting domestically and internationally for clients such as Clinique, Nike, Patreon, Converse and many more. My portfolio of work has helped organisations to become more consumer-centric, by bringing to life the needs of their consumers, as well as strategically aligning organisations. In addition to being an Advisory Board Member for DSOU, I’m currently a Mentor for Creative Access, an organisation that helps young people from under-represented backgrounds break into creative industries. When I’m not consulting or mentoring, you can listen to my monthly radio show, Oya Let's Chat, on Run Dem Radio, which centres change-makers, creators and artists who share stories about the music that has helped to shape their journey so far.
