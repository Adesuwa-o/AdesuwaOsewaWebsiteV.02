About

I'm Adesuwa

Problem Solver. Radio Presenter. DJ.

I’m a Problem Solver at creative management consultancy B+A, consulting domestically and internationally for clients such as Clinique, Nike, Patreon, Converse and many more. My portfolio of work has helped organisations to become more consumer-focused, by bringing to life the needs of their consumers, as well as strategically aligning organisations. I'm also an Advisory Board Member for Don't Sleep On Us and a Mentor for Creative Access, an organisation that helps young people from under-represented backgrounds break into creative industries.You can also find me blending Afrobeats with Amapiano on some days, hosting immersive events or moderating panel discussions.
